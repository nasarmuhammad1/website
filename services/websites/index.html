the process

1

Digital Strategy

Firstly, we at Te4tech® start with an exploratory workshop to understand your organization, your goals, your customers, their expectations and behavior, and how your website factors into it all. We work closely with our clients and partners in order to come up with a strategy document that covers, user personas, storyboards, feature lists & findings, and wireframes

2

Design Phase

Once armed with all information, we’ll set to work designing the user interface for the entire website and both mobile and desktop. You will be able to see how the pages and designs fit together and experience the website before the building starts. We also creating prototypes in order for us to rapidly test and optimise the website flow before beginning development

3

Testing & deployment

Once the designs are approved, we’ll build the website based on the approach agreed upon at the start of the project. Our in-house development team takes an agile approach to ensure you see tangible progress throughout the project. Our websites are built to last — and you’ll find it easy to manage content and updates in the back-end.
