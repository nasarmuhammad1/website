New Home. More Services. Bigger Team

Always ready to push the boundaries, especially when it comes to our own platform, we used our analytical eye to create a site that was visually engaging and also optimised for maximum performance. It also perfectly reflects the journey we’ve been through during these years. Let’s still consider it pretty much like a work in progress as we have yet to release plenty of case studies from product design to other digital campaigns, through more experiences, but we can now draw a line in the sand with this new milestone.

Real-world experience in all the right areas

The company was first led as a web agency, building memorable and engaging digital experiences . Fast forward a couple of years, we start building digital products and digital marketing campaigns for some of the largest companies on the island.  

Time passes by, and milestones too : First major international recognition with our very own website and its Site of The Day, Best UI Design, Best UX Design & Best Innovation on CSS Design Awards, Site of The Day with District on CSS Design Awards, 5 international web design awards including a Mobile Excellence award from Awwwards in collaboration with Google for the work we did with AB Finance, and 3 international web design awards on CSS Design Awards (Best in UI, Best in UX, Best in Innovation) and 1 international web design award – Honourable Mention on Awwwards for the work we did with Mercury Towers by Zaha Hadid Architects, growth, bigger team, more awards, more international collaborations, and finally opening of our new offices in the beginning of 2019.

Now, over time, Bison Studio made itself a name for its high-quality crafted projects and its high expertise in building emotionally engaging, user-centered digital experiences (websites), incredible digital products and strategic digital advertising campaigns that have a proven track record of achieving great results
