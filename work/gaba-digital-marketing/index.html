Search Engine Optimisation

Our thought process:

  • Conduct keywords research to identify what terms and phrases people search for on Google when searching for our client's relevant products. 
  • Conduct competitor analysis to identify what keywords our clients' main competitors are ranking for and what is required to overtake. 
  • Implement the necessary SEO tools on clients’ website to make it rank for the most searched for keywords related to their business. 

Te4tech has helped a lot of organisations, brands and companies rise to the first page of Google Search Engine and their websites by implementing a well-researched SEO strategy. After a few months of fine-tuning, the sites ranked in the first three places on the first page of Google for the search-phrases ‘The common keywords related to our clients' businesses’ were ranked on the first page for several other related keywords and phrases related to the businesses of our partners. 

Paid Advertising

Our thought process:

  • Building customer profiles that are based on data from client’s past customers and used it to create a plan for growing and scaling the brand’s target personas.
  • Setting up unique tracking links to be able to track the performance of campaigns. 
  • Adding the Facebook pixel to the websites for re-marketing purposes. Once this is set up, we then targeted those individuals who’d visit the site through a social media advertising campaign. 
  • Using our data insight to establish lookalike audiences on social media platforms (Facebook & Instagram) and use this to target new customers that are more likely to take action.
  • Building detailed strategies & and implementing several targeted Social Media and Google campaigns
